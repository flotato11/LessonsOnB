Welcome to Lessons on B

High Quality Lessons in a Positive Environment

Established in 1990, we are a music school with well-seasoned, experienced, independent music teachers, who offer music lessons on various instruments. We promote the love of music and the arts in blending education and pleasure. Our students thrive in beautiful surroundings. We teach all ages – early childhood, youth, teens, adults, and seniors!
